Privacy Policy for Aawin

Aawin is a task manager app that works entirely locally on your device. We respect your privacy and want you to understand how your data is handled.

Data Collection

Aawin does not collect, send, or share any personal information. All data, including your tasks, classes, layers, and schedules, is stored locally on your device.

Use of Data

Since the app stores everything locally, your data remains private and is never transmitted over the internet.

Sharing Data

We do not share your data with any third parties.
